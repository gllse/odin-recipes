Instant noodles

This is a recipe for how to make instant noodles, as found on every package of instant noodles in like, forever.

Ingredients:

  • noodles
  • water (preferably boiling)

How to make them:

  1. Go to your local noodles store, purchase whatever bag of noodles that you like
  2. Go home again
  3. Boil water in a pan
  4. Open the noodles package and remove the small spice bag from inside
  5. Put the instant noodles in the boiling water pan
  6. Add the spices, stir, enjoy! (Chopsticks optional, forks works as well)
