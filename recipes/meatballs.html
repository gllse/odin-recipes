Meatballs

This is a recipe for how to make meatballs, as found on allrecipes.

Ingredients:

  • Ground meat
  • Seasonings and herbs
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Italian bread
  • Water
  • Olive oil

How to make them:

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add bread crumbs and slowly add water
  3. Shape them into balls
  4. Fry them until done

What goes with meatballs?

The answer is simple: PASTA!!
