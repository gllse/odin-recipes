Classic lasagna

This is a recipe for how to make a classic lasagna, as found on MODERNhoney.

Ingredients for:

- Lasagna

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 32-40 ounce jar marinara sauce

- Parmesan ricotta cheese filling

  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps for:

- Lasagna

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Heat olive oil in medium heat
  3. Add onion and cook for 3-4 minutes
  4. Add ground beef and increase heat t0 medium high
  5. Sprinkle with garlic powder, salt and pepper
  6. Set heat to low and add marinara sauce

- Parmesan ricotta cheese filling

  1. Stir together ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs, basil, salt and pepper in a small bowl
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  3. Spread the bottom of a 9x13 baking pan with 1/4 cup of the meat sauce. Place 3 noodles in single layers on top of the sauce. Spread each noodle with parmesan ricotta cheese filling and sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and meat sauce. Repeat.
  4. Bake for 15-40 minutes in the oven
